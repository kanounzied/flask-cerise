    ACCIDENTAL DAMAGE FROM RIOTS AND NATURAL DISASTERS

    If your vehicle is damaged following a natural disaster or riot, we will pay you compensation.

    5000dt

    MEDICAL EXPENSES

    Reimbursement of medical expenses (hospitalization, outpatient treatment and revalidation).

    500dt

    What's covered

    FIRE

    In the event of a fire claim, we will refund the vehicle's current value.

    NATURAL DISASTERS

    Your vehicle is covered due to any natural disaster.

    CIVIL LIABILITY

    Whatever the nature of your activity, you are exposed to risks that may engage your civil liability. Don't worry, you're covered.

    CRIME AND VANDALISM

    We cover your vehicle in case it is stolen or damaged.

    What's not covered

    TECHNICAL BREAKDOWNS

    Technical breakdowns coverage is not included in our base policy.

    ADDED ACCESSORIES

    Let’s say you want to personalize your car and you’ve bought a new music system and a nice DVD screen. These items will not be covered by your car insurance policy because they are categorized as personal items.

    DRIVING AN UNOWNED VEHICLE

    If you drive a different car and get into an accident, you will not be covered. If that car is already on another insurance policy, you should be covered.

    PERSONAL BELONGINGS INSIDE THE VEHICLE

    If your car is stolen or damaged, as well as your personal items that you may have left inside, those items will not be covered.
