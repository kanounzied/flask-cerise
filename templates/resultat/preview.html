    Choose Your Deductible

    Deductible:
    This is the amount that will be deducted from any claim payment you may receive. For example, choosing a 1,000dt deductible means that if you file a claim for 5,000dt and it's approved, we will pay you 4,000dt.

    What's covered

    FIRE AND SMOKE

    A faulty Christmas light or your attempt at deep frying can cause some smoke and fire damage. We cover you for both.

    BAD WEATHER

    Wind, lightning, and hail are scary. You're covered for all of them. Flood insurance isn't part of the basic policy.

    WATER DAMAGE

    Your pipes may be feeling a chill, or your neighbor's bathtub is leaking through your ceiling. Aka, if there's sudden or accidental water damage, you're covered.

    CRIME AND VANDALISM

    Your home is full of stuff: furniture, clothing, appliances. We cover you if any of it is stolen or damaged.

    What's not covered

    EARTHQUAKE

    Earthquake coverage is not included in our base policy.

    HIGH-RISK DOG ATTACKS

    If your dog bites someone you're covered as long as they're not considered a high-risk breed, and don't have a history of attacks.

    FLOOD

    We do not cover structural or personal property damage caused by floods.

    ROOMMATES

    Our policy does not cover your roommates' stuff or personal injuries.
